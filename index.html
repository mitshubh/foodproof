Why Blockchain ?

A decentralized network of servers operating through smart constracts makes the data flow transparent and accessible in real time. Its a win-win situation for all parties as all the suppliers, processors, distributors, retailers and consumers are able to ensure that products meet quality and safety requirements.

Open New Doors

Seize untapped potential by redesigning network and planning routes by maximize sales and efficiency, which reducing food waste at the same time..

Smart Inventory Routing

Obtain a more granular forecasting of customer needs by looking at flow rate in the supply chain network, while simultaneously accounting for external factors in the marketplace, resulting in more satisfied customers and fewer lost sales.

Predict The Customer's Needs

Obtain a more granular forecasting of customer needs by looking at flow rate in the supply chain network, and thus effectively re-routing goods across the network.

Quick Traceability

The end-to-end tracking and traceability enable store chains, food brands and goods supply networks to quickly identify points of origin and distribution if it’s discovered that food is contaminated. This facilitates rapid mitigation of an emergency situation.

Unified Tracking

A unified tracking system assists distributors and other nodes in the network for security compliance. Also assist governmental agencies in food regulations and other checks.
