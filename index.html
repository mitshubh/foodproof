How can you trust your food source?

'Food fraud' is the deliberate substitution, dilution, addition, or misrepresentation of food, food ingredients, or food packaging.

43% of "organic" labeled items contained prohibited pesticides, and weren't truly organic afterall. In 2013, horse meat was found in burgers and other meat products labeled as 'beef'.

Consumers want to know food's origins

A recent survey found that 68% of consumers say they are not provided with enough information about their food and its origins, with 92% saying it was important to know this information. Shockingly, over one-fifth of consumers admit they are not at all knowledgeable about where their food comes from.
